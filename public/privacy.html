Ride Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 18, 2025

Ride helps you plan and share trips. We collect only what is necessary to provide the service and never sell your data.

Data We Collect

  • Account info: email and name from your chosen sign-in provider (Google, Microsoft, or Facebook).
  • Trip content you create: trips, waypoints, notes, attachments, and sharing settings.
  • Technical data: basic device/browser info and request logs for reliability and security.

How We Use Data

  • Authenticate you and secure your account.
  • Store and sync your trips and related content.
  • Show shared trips when you enable public sharing.
  • Maintain service reliability, prevent abuse, and improve user experience.

Sharing

  • Your trips stay private unless you explicitly enable public sharing. Public shares expose only the fields you choose (waypoints, route, public notes, cover image).
  • We do not sell personal data.
  • We may share data with service providers strictly to operate Ride.

Data Retention

  • Your content is retained while your account is active.
  • You can request deletion at any time (see Data Deletion below).

Your Choices

  • You can disable public sharing for any trip.
  • You can delete trips, notes, and attachments you own.
  • You can request full account deletion (see Data Deletion below).

Data Deletion

To delete your account and all associated data, submit a request at /deletion.html. We will erase your account, trips, notes, attachments, and sessions.

Contact

If you have questions, contact us at support@ride.incitat.io.
