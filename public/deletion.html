Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: December 18, 2025

You can delete your Ride account and all associated data (trips, notes, attachments, sessions) at any time.

Request Deletion

Email us at support@ride.incitat.io from the email address used for your Ride account. Include the subject line “Ride Data Deletion Request.”

We will verify ownership and delete your data within 30 days, usually much sooner.

What Gets Deleted

  • Your account and authentication linkage.
  • All trips, waypoints, notes, and attachments you own.
  • All active sessions.

What Stays

  • Minimal operational logs retained briefly for security and abuse prevention.
  • If you shared a trip publicly and others cached the link/data, we cannot control third-party caches.

If you have questions, contact support@ride.incitat.io.
